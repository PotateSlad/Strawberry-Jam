Header

Strawberry Jam is a group of musical friends based in Ankeny, Iowa. Our music can be generally described as "thought-provoking," "interesting," and sometimes terrible, although we do have a growing amount of original songs on this site alongside our covers. The band can oftentimes be found performing around the countryside or, alternatively, at AHS open mic nights. To see currently scheduled events, see our calendar. Feel free to also contact us to schedule an event or just say hi and proclaim your undying allegiance to our legion.

Strawberry Jam originated as a 10th grade web development assignment. When faced with the indecision over created a website about "anything," the creator decided to bring Strawberry Jam into being. The Mk. 1 website contained an extensive amount of unofficial members, who proved to be highly disposible in the long run. After the events of the War, when the ranks of Strawberry Jam experienced great dissension over how to live, the remaining members were coalesced here on the Mk. 2 website. These remaining members came together once more to play music, now much more effectively.

Please enjoy your stay.

'Cole

Height: 5'6

Favorite Member of the Band: Ian's Hair

Plays: Vocals

Cole was born in a small Russian village in the depths of Siberia, where she grew up fighting bears and Germans. During the cold, harsh winters of Siberia, it was music--and vodka--that kept her warm and in good spirits. At the tender age of eleven, Cole packed her bags and said farewell to her guardian Yeti to barefoot travel to America, where she drank her way to rock bottom. There, she met the other founding members of Strawberry Jam and the bonds of everlasting friendship were born.
